Hey Sailors!

Sail is almost upon us and we are so excited for you to experience a day of CS@Illinois! Here is a quick to do list so that you’re ready for Sail.

  1. Sign our consent form. To ensure everyone’s safety, we require that you fill out this form before the event.
  2. Sign up for buses: If you would like to sign up for a bus to take you to campus, please sign up using the following form. Seats fill up quickly, so sign up as soon as possible!
  3. Sign your parents up! If your parents would like to attend Sail, they can register here for the Sail Parent Track. They may also sign up for buses as a chaperone. The link for parent sign ups for buses is here.
  4. Sign up for courses: To sign up for courses, please use the following link. Make sure to sign up for one class per time slot (3 total)! Classes will be held from 10:00AM-10:50 AM, 11:00-11:50 AM, and 1:00PM-2:00 PM.
  5. Finally, take a look at our day-of guide to make sure you are ready for the day! Make sure to bring a laptop, pencil, and paper. Everything else, including food, will be provided. We can’t wait to see you there!

For any questions or concerns, email us at sail.cs.illinois@gmail.com.

We can’t wait to see you there!!
